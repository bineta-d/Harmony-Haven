Hobbies & Extracurriculars
For Student Athletes

Immersed in the demanding commitments of academics and sports, student athletes often develop tunnel vision, neglecting all other passions or interests beyond these realms. This intense confinement to solely two activities that are both mentally and physically demanding can lead to isolation from activities that once brought joy, disruptiopn of their social and personal balance and increased the risk of burnout- a state many athletes are familiar with. Recognizing the importance of maintaining separate interests and pastimes outside of academics and athletics is crucial. Diversifying interests and engaging with communities beyond the athletis or academic setting as college athletes can prevent burnout, make for a more well-rounded lifestyle, and contribute to a more fulfilling college experience. Explore the resources below for additional perspectives on time management and productivity which will allow for time to explore outside interests, achieve a social balance and allow athletes to open their often-closed off world one step at a time.
