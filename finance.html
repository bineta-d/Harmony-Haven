Financial Literacy
For Student Athletes

Student athletes, in their relentless pursuit of excellence on both the athletic field and in the classroom, often find themselves consumed by the demands of the present, leaving little time for considerations of their future financial well-being. The dedicated hours devoted to training and academic commitments can inadvertently sideline essential life skills, notably financial literacy. Many student athletes face challenges when transitioning to the real world, as they may lack the necessary knowledge to navigate subjects like taxes, loans, and credit responsibly. Understanding the importance of financial literacy becomes paramount in ensuring a successful transition beyond the realms of sports and academics. Equipping student athletes with the tools to comprehend and manage their finances is crucial for fostering independence and setting the stage for a secure financial future. Recognizing this need, below are resources that offer valuable insights and guidance, serving as a starting point for anyone embarking on the journey to financial literacy.
