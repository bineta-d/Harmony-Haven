About The Haven

Objective

The objective of The Haven is to host information that teaches student athletes at any level about the importance create a balanced lifestyle in which they can make informed decisions about mental & physical health, sports, professional development, money, hobbies, nutrition and more.

Inspiration

This project is inspired by my love of learning and my admiration for the work ethic and commitment demonstrated by student-athletes all over the world, something that is worthy of the highe commendation.
I recognize from first-hand experience that as athletes dedicate ample time to the sport they love, it can often lead to a personal imbalance as they invest all their energy and personal resources into only our sport causing the athlete to neglect other fundamental aspects of their life such as mental health, nutrition, relationships, professional development and more.
My goal is to create a safe informative platform where student athletes can find information about all the ways to take care of themselves outside of their sport in order to maintain a balanced lifestyle and fulfilling lifestyle whilst becoming a well-rounded individual.
